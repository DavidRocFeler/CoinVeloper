Blockchain Researcher

Hailstone Labs · NAMER

Remote . full time . Mid-Senior level

11-50 employees · IT Services and IT Consulting

Skills: Investigación técnica, Publicaciones académicas, +4 more

About the job

We are looking for a Blockchain Researcher to join our team. You will play a crucial role in exploring and analyzing the latest advancements in Blockchain technologies and their potential applications. Your research efforts will contribute to the development of innovative solutions and strategies for our company. If you have experience in driving technical research in Web3, come and join our team!

Responsibilities
  1. Conduct in-depth research on Web3 technologies, different Web3 projects and related emerging trends, with research topics including but not limited to Consensus algorithms, L2 designs (prover coordination and sequencer design, decentralization, incentivization. Levels of finality; L2 governance, PoS vs PoG; auctioning the right to build a block etc), Protocol-side infrastructure for blockspace markets , Fee markets and blockchain resource pricing, Economic models of user demand and for privacy, Contract theory and pricing models for order flow, Incentive compatibility of cryptoeconomic mechanisms, Ordering protocols and block building primitives etc
  2. Stay updated with the latest developments in the Web3 ecosystem.
  3. Analyze and assess the feasibility, scalability, and security of various Web3 technologies and protocols.
  4. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify and define research objectives, requirements, and milestones.
  5. Design and execute experiments, simulations, and prototypes to evaluate the performance and potential use cases of Web3 technologies.
  6. Generate comprehensive reports, whitepapers, and technical documentation summarizing research
  7. findings, methodologies, and recommendations.
  8. Contribute to the development and optimization of Web3-related projects, providing technical insights and guidance.
  9. Provide technical expertise and support to internal teams, stakeholders, and clients on Web3 concepts, technologies, and implementation strategies.
  10. Assist in identifying and evaluating partnerships and collaboration opportunities within the Web3 community.
  11. Participate in conferences, workshops, and industry events to share research findings, network with experts, and stay at the forefront of Web3 advancements.
Requirements
  1. Bachelor in Computer Science, Engineering, Physics, Mathematics, Economics, or a related field. A Ph.D. is a plus. Knowledge about game theory is a plus.
  2. A strong understanding of blockchain technologies and distributed protocol design, including blockchain, smart contracts, decentralized storage, and distributed consensus algorithms. Particularly, a demonstrable ability to identify the trade-offs faced by protocols when deployed on blockchains.
  3. Steep learning curve to catch up the most updated development in the space.
  4. Basic understanding of topics such as single-slot finality, pre-confirmations, execution tickets, Ethereum scaling in view of (i) state growth, (ii) history growth, (iii) state access, and (iv) the gas limit, etc.
  5. Proficiency in programming languages commonly used in the space, such as Solidity, Rust, or JavaScript.
  6. Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to evaluate and interpret complex technical concepts and research papers.
  7. Strong written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to present technical information in a clear and concise manner.
  8. Familiarity with popular blockchain platforms and related tooling.
  9. Ability to work effectively both independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced, dynamic environment.
  10. Passion for blockchain technology, decentralized systems, and the vision of Web3.
