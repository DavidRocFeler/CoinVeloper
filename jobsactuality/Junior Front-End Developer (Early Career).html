About Nascent

Founded in 2020, Nascent exists to build, expand, and capture opportunity, in open markets and permissionless technologies. Building from a base of permanent capital, we deploy assets across a range of both liquid and long-term strategies that ensure we are among the most active users of the open financial system we are helping to build. We’ve made venture investments in 50+ early-stage teams that we believe have the potential to create substantive change, expand boundaries, and find new horizons. We are consistently on the hunt for the most competitive and curious minds exploring the edges of the crypto ecosystem and leverage our hyper flexible structure to accelerate ideas into fully deployed strategies.

The Opportunity

As a Junior Front-End Developer on the Nascent team you will play a critical role in building and deploying front-end code to our trading platform. From designing high impact and scalable components to solving complex technical and design problems you will serve as an integral partner to our trading team. Working closely with our trading group and engineering team, you’ll have an opportunity to exercise creativity and technical skill in solving meaningful problems, empowering our trading team to thrive at the forefront of the crypto ecosystem.

This role is a unique skillset, requiring a blend of technical expertise, effective collaboration skills, and an understanding and appreciation of financial markets and blockchain technology. This is a high-impact team, designed to advance the strategies that make our team successful. The ideal candidate is early in their career, curious and eager to contribute to building the future global economic system.

Responsibilities
  1. Architect, design and build the next phase of Nascent’s ecosystem, enabling our team to do their best work.
  2. Ensure resilience and stability through quality code reviews, unit, regression and user acceptance testing.
  3. Be an integral contributor to a lean agile development team, fostering a culture of continuous improvement.
  4. Operate independently and in unison with a team to deliver code updates partnership pipelines, conversion rates, and revenue impact.
