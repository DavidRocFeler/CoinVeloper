About prePO

prePO is the decentralized trading platform for pre-public assets - pre-stocks, pre-tokens, points, and more.

Our mission is to democratize pre-public investing - transforming a world of financial exclusion into a world of permissionless access.

Our Values

At prePO, the following team values describe the people we want to work with (and the people you would want to work with too!):

  1. Passion: being engaged and enthusiastic about the work, the mission, and making an effective impact on the world.
  2. Autonomy: being proactive, resourceful, and making decisions in prePO’s best interest.
  3. Overcommunication: sharing feedback and all relevant information - directly, frequently, clearly and empathetically.
  4. High Performance: producing high-quality outcomes with speed and consistency, and continuously raising the bar for yourself and others. Focus: being laser-focused on high-priority, high-impact tasks that work us towards achieving prePO’s mission.
  5. Incrementalism: delivering value via incremental iterations.
  6. Simplicity: seeking out the simplest solutions.
  7. Innovation: embracing creativity, and leading the industry with forward-thinking ideas.
  8. Decentralization: valuing solutions which are trustless, permissionless and automated.
The Role

The “Senior” title on the job description is purely for describing the experience level of the hire we’re looking for. Our job titles don’t include hierarchical seniority systems and we use consensus for driving decisions.prePO is looking for a Senior Solidity Smart Contract Engineer to take ownership over the development of novel DeFi-related smart contracts.

You will have the opportunity to:
  1. Drive high-level decisions about the prePO smart contract architecture.
  2. Write well-documented, performant, clean, and re-usable Solidity code.
  3. Collaborate with product owners, frontenders, and other developers.
  4. Work at a rapid pace with a large amount of ownership and autonomy.
The Ideal Candidate
  1. have 4+ years experience as a Software Engineer.
  2. have 2+ years experience as Solidity Smart Contract Engineer.
  3. have an advanced knowledge of Git, GitHub, TypeScript, Foundry.
  4. have complementary engineering skills in DevOps and backend development.
  5. have significant experience writing comprehensive smart contract test suites.
  6. have a strong working knowledge of Solidity design patterns and Solidity security best practices.
  7. have significant experience collaborating within an engineering team and reviewing code.
  8. have exceptional technical communication skills (written and verbal).
  9. have an in-depth knowledge of economics, finance, and the financial sector.
  10. have an in-depth and up-to-date practical knowledge of the DeFi (and broader crypto) landscape.

We are committed to fostering diversity and inclusiveness within our organization, and we strongly encourage candidates of all backgrounds to apply.

Note that we cannot offer visa sponsorships at this time.

Benefits & Perks
  1. Fully-remote work with flexible working hours.
  2. Work on the bleeding edge of DeFi and crypto.
  3. Learn from other senior high-performing team members.
  4. Team off-sites and sponsored conference/hackathon trips.
  5. Budget for learning / professional development / subscriptions.
  6. Join a force for good - prePO has made a pledge to dedicate at least 2% of time, product, and profit towards charity.

We look forward to your application!
