Cryptocurrency Investor 'Mr. 100' Resurfaces with Major Bitcoin Purchase

The enigmatic cryptocurrency investor known as 'Mr. 100' has recently made a substantial Bitcoin purchase, sparking interest within the crypto community. The transaction involved the acquisition of 4,100 BTC, amounting to a total of $238 million, at a time when each coin was valued at $58,000.

This purchase marks the first activity from the wallet since the recent Bitcoin halving, according to Bitinfocharts data. Analysts are closely monitoring Mr. 100's wallet due to its distinctive operations, often involving the purchase of exactly 100 BTC. Notably, this recent transaction follows a period of dormancy since April 19, just before the halving event.

Prior to this hiatus, Mr. 100 garnered attention for consistently purchasing 100 BTC daily, a pattern initiated on February 14. With holdings totaling approximately 61,150 BTC, Mr. 100's wallet now ranks twelfth among Bitcoin market participants, boasting an estimated value exceeding $3.85 billion.

Renewed Activity Sparks Speculation

The resurgence of activity from Mr. 100 has fueled speculation within the crypto sphere, with some analysts interpreting it as a potential precursor to a Bitcoin price surge. The recent purchase at $58,000 per BTC has already yielded a substantial profit, with Bitcoin's value rising by nearly 10% to its current price of $63,700.

This surge has translated into a profit of almost $24 million from a single transaction for Mr. 100. Across the entire portfolio, the estimated gain now exceeds $1.4 billion. Despite ongoing curiosity surrounding the wallet's owner, past associations with various cryptocurrency exchanges have yielded inconclusive evidence regarding ownership.
